Who are we?

A young group of tech enthusiasts & programmers that share a common ideology regarding the potential of humanity and technology.

Tilhr Tech was first created to change the way people view technology. After months of small updates & gradual evolution we decided to publish tilhr.tech to the web in hope to find a collective of like-minded individuals who are passionate about contributing to the #tech-community. We are actively trying to relate the benefits of the 4th Industrial Revolution to a wider audience through community projects, articles, applications, big data analytics, & free open-source resources.

Don't work for technology; make technology work for you.
