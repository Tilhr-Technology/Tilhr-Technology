What is Tilhr Technology?

Our website was first created testing some code for a school project. We are proud to say that tilhr.tech is in the works to becoming a full fledged technology hub- designed specifically for developers & young learners. Our website is still in beta.

Updates will continue to roll out.
After 1,000 users join, we will develop a mobile app for tilhr.tech.
