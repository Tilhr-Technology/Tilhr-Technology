  • How can I speed up my device?

    No matter what O.S. (operating system) you are running, speeding up a device is fairly easy. Save all of your files to a seperate storage device & use the device's restore option. If the device does not have a built in restore function, a factory-reset will work even bettter. If this does not solve your problem, your device is probably slowing down due to a hardware issue. If this is the case, you need to contact your manufacturer & replace the appropriate parts. If you are having hardware issues with a PC, send us a detailed explenation of exactly what you are experiencing.
